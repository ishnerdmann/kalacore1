Who we are (and why that matters)

Finally, meet the vision behind the studio!

We are a dedicated collective of artists and educators based in NIFT, driven by a love for foundational mastery and creative action. We are proud to be an institute affiliated with Skill India and NSDPI , ensuring our curriculum meets the highest national standards. Over the years, we've built a sanctuary where classical techniques meet contemporary vision, guiding hundreds of students across fine arts, fashion, and visual storytelling.

That same drive to communicate clearly and explore new perspectives shows up in how we teach, and how we live. We speak the language of oils, charcoal, and fabric, learning the nuances of artistic soul every single day — one brushstroke at a time ;)

Artistic expression has always been our core — from childhood sketches scattered in studios to designing full collections and hosting curation workshops. When we're not teaching, you'll find us capturing the soul of the city on canvas, exploring street textures, or vibing with our creative community as we’re always drawn to creative momentum.

Because, at the heart of it all, we love helping artistic visions move faster, clearer, and with profound depth. When pieces click, your vision becomes undeniable.
